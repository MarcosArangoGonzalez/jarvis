# BJJ Learning Videos

This area is for videos and social captures saved to learn Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu as a sport.

It is not the `bjj-app` project knowledge base. App implementation, video-analysis architecture, RAG, Java/Python contracts, and TFG material belong in [[bjj-app-index]].

## Categories

- `whatsapp/` - BJJ videos and technique material captured from the WhatsApp backlog.
- `social-captures/` - Instagram/social captures saved as sport-learning references.
